# Conic Sections

## Lesson 1: Distance and Midpoint Formula

Distance Formula <br>
$\frac{x_{2}+x_{1}}{2}, \frac{y_{2}+y_{1}}{2}$ <br>
Midpoint Formula <br>
$D=\sqrt{(x_{2}-x_{1})^2+{(y_{2}-y_{1}})^2}$ <br>
---

## Lesson 2: Circles

General Form: <br>
$x^2+y^2+Dx+Ey+F=0$ <br>
Standard Form: <br>
$(x-h)^2+(y-k)^2=r^2$ <br>
---

## Lesson 3: Hyperbolas

Horizontal: <br>
$\frac{(x-h)^2}{a^2}-\frac{(y-k)^2}{b^2}=1$ <br>
Vertical: <br>
$\frac{(y-k)^2}{a^2}-\frac{(x-h)^2}{b^2}=1$ <br>
Center to Focus: <br>
$c^2=a^2+b^2$ <br>
<br>
Horizontal/Vertical boundaries: $\sqrt{a}/\sqrt{b}$

### Graphing hyperbolas

- Find and plot a center point <br>
- Find the c value <br>
- Use a and b to sketch a rectangle to help find vertices and asymptotes <br>
- Use c to find each focus <br>
- Sketch the hyperbola <br>
