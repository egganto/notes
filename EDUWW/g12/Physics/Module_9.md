## Calorimetry & Specific Heat

## Phase Changes & Latent Heat

Equation for Latent Heat:
$$Q=mL$$
$Q=$ heat/energy <br>
$m=$ mass <br>
$L=$ latent heat <br>
<br>
Fusion: use $L_{f}$ (solid <-> liquid) <br>
Vaporization: use $L_{v}$ (liquid <-> gas) <br>
